## 2026-06-26

## New features

- The login page now shows only a "Sign in with Google" button — there is no email/password form, no other social login options (Microsoft, Apple, Facebook), and no "Create an account" link.

## 2026-06-26

## New features

- **Account menu in the header** — clicking your avatar now opens a dropdown showing your display name and email address, along with a "Log out" button. Clicking anywhere outside the dropdown closes it without affecting your session.
- **Log out** — clicking "Log out" in the account menu ends your session and brings you to the login page. If something goes wrong on the server side, you'll still be sent to the login page and your session will expire on its own shortly after.
- **Protected pages stay protected after logout** — navigating to any page that requires sign-in after logging out will redirect you to the login page automatically.
